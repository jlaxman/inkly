# Product Image Guidelines

## Image Requirements

All product images should follow these specifications for consistency and professionalism:

### Model Requirements
- **Model**: Stylish American/white male, approximately 30s-40s
- **Appearance**: Short brown hair, pleasant smile, professional look
- **Style**: Clean, modern, approachable

### Background Requirements
- **Style**: Clean, minimalistic backgrounds similar to Flipkart product pages
- **Setting**: Brightly lit indoor studio or office environment
- **Colors**: Neutral tones (white, light gray, beige)
- **Focus**: Product should be the main focus, background should not distract

### Product Requirements
- **Custom Logos**: All products must clearly show custom logos/branding
- **Visibility**: Logo should be clearly visible and well-positioned
- **Quality**: High-resolution images suitable for e-commerce (minimum 800x800px)
- **Multiple Angles**: Each product should have 2-3 images showing different angles

### Product Categories & Image Types

#### Apparels (T-Shirts, Polo, Hoodies, Caps)
- Model wearing the product
- Logo clearly visible on chest/back
- Product shown in natural wearing position
- Clean background with good lighting

#### Drinkware (Mugs, Flasks, Bottles)
- Product held by model or displayed on clean surface
- Custom logo visible on product
- Product shown from multiple angles
- Professional product photography style

#### Business Stationery (Cards, Letterheads, Notebooks)
- Product displayed on clean desk/table
- Logo visible on product
- Professional office setting
- Clean, organized presentation

#### Photo Gifts (Frames, Prints, PhotoBooks)
- Product displayed elegantly
- Custom photo/logo visible
- Premium presentation
- Clean background

#### Rewards & Recognition (Awards, Trophies, Certificates)
- Product displayed prominently
- Engraving/logo clearly visible
- Professional presentation
- Premium feel

#### Packaging (Boxes, Bags, Stickers)
- Product displayed with logo visible
- Clean presentation
- Professional styling
- Logo clearly shown

#### Marketing & Promo (Banners, Brochures, Standees)
- Product displayed professionally
- Custom design visible
- Clean background
- Professional presentation

## Image Generation Tips

1. **Use AI Image Generation**: Tools like DALL-E, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion
2. **Prompt Template**: 
   ```
   "Professional product photo of [product name] with custom logo, 
   stylish American male model in 30s-40s, clean white background, 
   e-commerce style, high quality, 8k resolution"
   ```

3. **Consistency**: Maintain consistent style across all product images
4. **Logo Placement**: Ensure logo is clearly visible and well-positioned
5. **Lighting**: Use professional studio lighting for best results
